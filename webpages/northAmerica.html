North America


North America is arguably home to the most popular world music sub-genre, Salsa. Salsa music orignally arose in New York City, however this genre was much further evolved in Cuba. Music from Belize has strong influences from many different countries. Considering it is such a small country Belize has an exceptional range of music styles and traditions. Explore traditional and popular music from both of these countries through this website, and learn about the roots of many popular songs you may have previously listend to and enjoyed.
