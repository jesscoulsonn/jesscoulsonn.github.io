Belize

Despite what may be from expected from a country that is as small as Belize, Belize is home to an expceptional range of music styles and traditions. The music from Belize has strong influences of Creole, Mestizo, Garifun and Mayan. Many Belizean artists and performers have achieved international recagnition, some particularly noteable artists include Andy Palacio, Lova Boy, and Paul Nabor.

Garifuna Music

The Garifuna culture within Belize displays the wide variety of African influences on their music and overall culture. This similarity is extremely evident when comparing the music of indigenous Africa. One noteable difference is that the patterns found in Belizean music are less overlapping than those in many traditional songs found in Africa. Garifuna music relies heavily on the use of the drum, similar to many traditional African songs. Often the drumming patterns are intricate and require two (or even three) drummers.
