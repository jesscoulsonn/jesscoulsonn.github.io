What is World Music?



World music encompasses a wide variety of genres, it is a term that is often used to describe musical traditions, styles and genres that are non-Western. However, some forms of World Music have been heavily influenced by Western classical and pop music. Almost every country can provide their own example of World Music, each country has a different style that is potrayed through their music. Often cultural traditions are explored through music.

Streaming music is becomming increasingly popular, with more and more people choosing streaming over purchasing each day. In order to maintain and grow their audience World Music artists must provide their music on popular streaming platforms.
