Europe


Europe is not well known for their input into the world music genre. Despite this, many countries within Europe have had a huge influence on world music, shaping tunes and rythmns used in many popular songs. For many years, traditional European instruments have been used to create music, and even influence a wide variety of different countries and genres. Through this website you can explore and expand your knowledge of popular world music tracks from France and the UK. Both of these countries have a brilliant selection of world music for you to enjoy and learn more about, expanding your cultural knowledge.
